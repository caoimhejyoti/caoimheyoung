Who is Caoimhe?

I am a colourful amalgamation of creative arts and problem solver. I may be a fledgling Fullstack developer but that is not stopping me!

I am a keen coder and cannot wait to get stuck into the next project. Having worked in the Arts and Events industry in the UK, I moved to Perth, Australia in 2019 just in time for the pandemic.

Through the chaos of settling in my new home and a worldwide lockdown, my passion for coding was re-ignited. Having recently graduated from the UWA Full Stack Development Bootcamp, I knew there was still lots more to learn and am so grateful for Matrix Data sponsoring me on the She Codes Australia Plus Course.

With my coding skills and my background in the Arts and Events industry, I am looking forward to creating some exciting projects in the future. I am passionate about creating reality from imagination.

So what world shall we create next?
