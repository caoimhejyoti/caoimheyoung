Buddy Up

Project Overview

Buddy Up is a meeting platform developed to provide users a place to meet up with others for activities

Created as part of the UWA Bootcamp, the app was designed with gamers in mind. We wanted to provide users a space to meet up with others to play games.

Part of the design with this project was to allow it to develop and exzpand beyond gamers. We wanted to create a platform that could be used by individuals to meet like minded people and create friendships.
